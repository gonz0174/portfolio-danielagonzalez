Creating a chic website for an exclusive swimsuit boutique, offering luxurious and stylish beachwear for discerning shoppers seeking elegance and high fashion.

Website choosen:

Farfett is the name of my personal business that I chose for this project.

Who is Farfett?

Farfett is the epitome of upscale beachwear, a haven for those seeking sophistication in swimwear. We curate a collection of exquisite swimsuits, each piece a testament to high fashion and refined taste. Our online boutique is meticulously designed to reflect opulence and exclusivity, offering a handpicked selection of luxurious swimwear that embodies elegance and style. Dive into Farfett's world and discover a realm where luxury meets the waves.

Process of the website:

  • Easily to access to the products.
  • The first thing when the user goes to the website knows exaclty what sells.
  • Customer Service friendly.
  • Easily purchase.
  • Finding ideas of uniqueness - Icons.
  • Not overwhealming.

Website wireframes Lo-fi proposal:

Adobe XD were used for the creation of the wireframes for the website proposal. The idea was:

  • Focus in the idea of sell the products.
  • Friendly navigation through out the webpage.
  • Sections with real information and content:
      • New In: People know what are the new capsule.
      Beach Wear: Category for only beach clothes.
      Swimwear: Category only for swimsuits.
      Collections: Category that present new collection and previous ones.
      Sales: Category only for the clothes or accesories that are on sale.
      About Us: Important for generate sense of ownership of the brand.
      Contact Us: Important for create calmness when making the purchase and if something were to occur .
