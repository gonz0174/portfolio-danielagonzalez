Project Description:

This project revolves around enhancing an existing website by proposing and implementing improvements. The primary goal is to elevate the original website by introducing a new and enhanced design or functionality to better meet the needs of its users or audience.

Website choosen:

This is the original Logo of Meow! That's Hot.

Who is Meow! That's hot?

It's a bar / restaurant that specializes in the sale of its exclusive line of artisanal hot sauces.

Process of the new design:

  • Knowing Meow! That's hot.
  • What products they sell?
  • What kind of vibes they are?
  • What we want to transmit to their clients?

Why the new proposal?

A good design can connect clients and feeling part of the brand, that's why:

  • The logo is not well thinking.
  • The website is not friendly.
  • The main purpose of the website is not sell or inform.

1. We create the moodboard:

Varoious moodboards were presented to the client, but he choose only one that has the key message that they want to convey wich are:

  • Vibrant: You can see it in the general color pallete.
  • Playful: You can see it in the round corners,the buttons, and the illustration.
  • Bold: You can see it in the black color pallete and fonts.

The process of the logo:

Various logos were create it, but only this one was choose. My creation was inspired in the black cat, and the hot of a chile!

Final result of the logo:

The client feel more appealing with my design and also because the black cat are their signature!
